About Us

We are a group of five undergraduate students from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, guided by Professor Mainack Mondal.

As part of our project to evaluate the impact of various factors on the identification of misinformation on social media, we are conducting this survey to learn how different people respond to misinformative posts online and how we can build tools to best assist the public in staying safe from misinformation.

Our study has been approved by the Institutional Review Board at IIT Kharagpur to be ethical and we take consent from you before you answer our misinformation and demographic related questions.

To protect you from possible misinformation during this survey, we provide you a detailed summary and resources for every social media post you come across during this survey. The survey would only require 5-10 minutes of your valuable time.

Click on 'Get Started' to start the survey process.

About the Survey



The survey questionnaire was developed by the student investigators and initially contains three questions that show you a social media post and ask you to identify if it is misinformative or not. The posts maybe misinformative or true in reality. In addition, we show you an "alert" stating it is misinformative or not misinformative, but it is upto you whether to believe it or not. In addition, we also ask you whether you have seen the post prior to the survey to identify any possible bias you may have.

Once the misinformation questions are answered, there will be a short demographic survey, asking your age group, gender, educational qualification and frequency of using social media. Afterwards you will be led to a thank-you page mentioning the details and explanation for the posts you've already seen in the survey, to clear up any questions you might have.

No personal information (except those shared by you) is collected as part of this project. Our research has been approved by the Institutional Review Board at Rutgers University. Please refer to our privacy policy for additional information.
